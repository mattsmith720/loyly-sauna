## What you are actually buying

A commercial traditional sauna is a $30,000 to $80,000 timber asset. Left to general cleaners, it is usually being actively damaged: bleach, ammonia and pressure-washing reactivate under heat and rot the bench undersides, which brings forward a $20,000 to $60,000 refurbishment by years. LOYLY CO. exists to defer that refurbishment, keep you inspection-ready, and protect your Google-review reputation. Our rates reflect a specialist trade, not a commodity cleaning run.
